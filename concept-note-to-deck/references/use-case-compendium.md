## Presentation Format Notes

Two primary formats used in the compendium. See [slide-formats.md](slide-formats.md) for full slide-by-slide layout of each.

- **Use Case format**: 4–6 slides. Solution-forward, lighter on customer context. Used for prospects and generalized demos.
- **Case Study format**: 5–8 slides. Evidence-forward, customer-specific (named or anonymized), metric-heavy. Includes a dedicated "Company/Pain Point/Approach" framing slide.
- **One-pager format**: Single dense slide. Company / Pain Point / Approach / Critical Business Issue / Key Challenges / How We Helped / KPIs. Used for quick reference or leave-behinds.
